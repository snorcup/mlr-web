Quick use

  1. Open in Chrome/Edge over HTTPS or localhost.
  2. Click Start audio, then load one or more audio files.
  3. Click pads in rows 1–7 to jump directly through slices 1–16.
  4. Record patterns with the P1–P4 Rec buttons, then loop them with Play.
  5. On hardware, bottom row columns 9–12 play patterns and columns 13–16 record patterns.
  6. Connect a monome classic via USB and choose it from the browser serial picker.
