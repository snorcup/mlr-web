Quick use

  1. Open in Chrome/Edge over HTTPS or localhost.
  2. Click Start audio, then load one or more audio files.
  3. Click pads in rows 1–7 to jump through slices 1–8; hold modifier for slices 9–16.
  4. Connect a monome classic via USB and choose it from the browser serial picker.
